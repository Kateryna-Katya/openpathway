TWO GAME MODES

Choose your path.

  • Classic Mode

    In Classic Mode, players are shown a path and must find it within a specified time.
    Each completed level unlocks the next one, allowing steady progression.
    If you exit the game, your progress is saved and you continue where you left off. This mode is all about staying consistent and slowly getting better.

  • Against the Clock Mode

    This mode gives you more time but resets from level one every session. The goal is to advance through levels as far as possible in one continuous run. Speed is basically the main thing you're fighting against. It’s perfect for players who thrive under pressure and enjoy pushing their limits.
